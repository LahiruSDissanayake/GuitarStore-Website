About Us

Guitar Center (Pvt) Ltd is a division of the Dissanayake Group of Companies in Sri Lanka. The Music shop first commenced business over 65 years ago in the rural township of kotte. In 1973, it moved to a busier location and found its permanent home in Nugegoda. Within just a short period of time Guitar Center expanded operations and activities in a network of Branches Island wide to better serve its customers. Today we are comprised of more than 10 branches including shops in some of the major cities in Sri Lanka such as Kandy, Matara, Gampaha and Colombo. Guitar Center (Pvt) Ltd which was established in 2000 is another subsidiary of the Dissanayake Group of Companies.
